Is there an admission fee to enter the open-air museum, or is it free?
Yes, there is an admission fee to enter the open-air museum. The fee is Rs 100 per person for entry without a guide. For school educational tours, which include a guide and transport, the price is reduced to Rs 70 per person. For regular visitors who wish to have a guide and transport, the fee is Rs 120 per person. All the money collected from admission fees will be used to fund the work done in the museum, including renovations, preservation, and other maintenance efforts.
Is photography allowed inside the museum?
Photography is allowed in certain areas of the museum, but it depends on the specific locations. We kindly ask all visitors to maintain respect and follow the guidelines posted at each spot they visit. Please check for signage or ask our staff if you're unsure about photography rules in a particular area.
Are there any recommended routes or itineraries for first-time visitors?
Yes, we have a ready-made app you can download. Inside, you'll find four different tracks to choose from for your visit. Plus, you'll receive a gift when you scan the QR codes found at various spots.
How can we book a guided tour in advance?
You can book a guided tour by visiting our website or contacting our staff services. Advanced booking is recommended, especially for large groups or school tours.
Is the museum accessible for visitors with disabilities?
We strive to make our museum accessible to all visitors. Most areas are wheelchair accessible, and we offer services such as sign language interpretation upon request. Please contact us in advance for any specific accessibility needs.
