Opening hours

MORIS Open-Air Museum welcomes visitors from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM on weekdays, and from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM on weekends. Please note that some historical sites, such as Champ de Mars, are not available on Saturdays. Additionally, visits to the inside of Line Barracks are only allowed upon special request.Please contact our staff members.

Guided tour and map

A map will be available at the counter. We also encourage you to download the MORIS app to participate in our interactive game and win prizes. The app serves as a map to guide you through the different tracks.

Food and Drink

No outside food is allowed. However, we encourage all visitors to support our local sellers and snack vendors found throughout the Port Louis Open-Air Museum. Enjoy a variety of local delicacies while helping to support the community.

Accessibility

Our museum is designed to be accessible for every visitors from children to person with disabilities.If you have specific accessibility needs, please contact us in advance to ensure a comfortable visit.
