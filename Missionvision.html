Mission

MORIS Open-Air Museum is dedicated to preserving and celebrating the rich cultural heritage and history of Mauritius. Our mission is to create an engaging and educational environment where visitors can explore and connect with the diverse stories and landmarks of Port Louis. Through interactive exhibits, guided tours, and community events, we strive to inspire a deeper appreciation for our shared past while fostering a sense of pride and unity among all who visit.

We are committed to:

Preservation: Safeguarding the historical sites and artifacts of Port Louis for future generations through careful conservation and restoration efforts.

Education: Providing educational programs and resources that enhance understanding and appreciation of the cultural, historical, and social significance of our exhibits.

Accessibility: Ensuring that our museum is welcoming and accessible to everyone, regardless of age, background, or physical ability.

Community Engagement: Collaborating with local communities, artists, and historians to create a vibrant and inclusive cultural space that reflects the diversity and spirit of Mauritius.

Together with colleagues, visitors, schools, groups, companies, heritage institutions, policy partners, administrations, nature organisations, and tourism partners, we make MORIS. Together with you.

Vision

Our vision is to transform MORIS our firt Open-Air Museum into a premier cultural destination that celebrates and preserves the rich heritage of Mauritius. We aspire to create a dynamic and interactive experience for all visitors, where history comes alive through innovative exhibits, and active community participation.

We envision a future where:

Cultural Heritage Thrives: Our historical sites and artifacts are preserved with care, ensuring they remain vibrant symbols of our island's diverse history.

Community Bonds Strengthen: We build strong connections with local communities, promoting a sense of pride and unity through collaborative events and initiatives.

Sustainable Practices Lead: We champion sustainability in all our efforts, supporting local businesses and implementing eco-friendly practices to protect our environment.

By achieving this vision, the MORIS Open-Air Museum will preserve the past and enrich cultural unity for future generations. We aim to inspire people worldwide, showcasing the unique heritage of Mauritius and fostering respect for the stories that shape us all.
